# Before you start

</header>

Before each production go-live, contact the FastStore team at least 4 weeks before the date and make sure that the following was complete:

<Steps>
  ### Account Edition

  Your account edition is set to `vtex.edition-store@5.x Edition` app in your master workspace.

  <Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
    If you do not have the appropriate Edition app installed, [open a support
    ticket](https://help.vtex.com/support) requesting the installation of the
    `vtex.edition-store@5.x Edition` app in your master workspace.
  </Callout>


  ### FastStore Onboarding

  The project was deployed during the [FastStore Onboarding](/docs/getting-started/1-faststore-onboarding/overview) process.


  ### Storefront development

  The storefront was developed during the [Setting Up the Project](/docs/getting-started/2-setting-up-the-project).

</Steps>
